About Meridian Academy

Helping Students Score Higher,
Every Year Since 2009

Over 1,20,000 Class 11 and 12 students have prepared with Meridian Academy for CBSE Boards, JEE, and NEET. Our results speak for themselves.

Meridian Academy was founded on a belief that quality exam preparation should not depend on geography or the coaching institute you have access to.

We build every course around the exam — not the textbook. Our faculty team, comprising IIT and AIIMS graduates alongside experienced school educators, designs content that explicitly bridges NCERT fundamentals with the depth and problem-solving style required by JEE Advanced and NEET UG.

Our approach is outcome-focused. We track each student's performance chapter by chapter, identify gaps early, and assign targeted practice rather than generic homework. Doubt resolution is treated as a priority — not an afterthought.

We have produced over 4,200 JEE and NEET selections, including students who secured ranks inside the top 500 in JEE Advanced. We publish our results annually with full transparency.

Whether you are a Class 11 student planning two years ahead, a Class 12 student managing board pressure alongside entrance exams, or a dropper targeting a rank improvement, our courses are structured to meet you where you are.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Concept First

We never teach shortcuts before concepts. Every chapter builds understanding from first principles so students can tackle any exam variation, not just familiar question types.

Exam-Mapped Content

Every lecture, test, and assignment is explicitly aligned to CBSE, JEE Main, JEE Advanced, or NEET UG patterns. Students always know exactly what they are preparing for.

Data-Driven Progress

We track performance at the question level. Weak chapters are identified automatically and targeted practice is assigned before they become gaps at exam time.

Accessible to All

Quality preparation should not be limited to students in big cities or those who can afford premium offline coaching. Our courses are priced to be genuinely accessible.

Doubt Resolution

Unresolved doubts compound into bigger gaps. Our faculty responds to every doubt within 4 hours on school days with detailed, personalised explanations.

Transparent Results

We publish our selection data annually — rank-wise, subject-wise, and college-wise. We do not inflate or cherry-pick. Our students deserve to see honest numbers.
